Frequently Asked Questions

Membership

How do I sign up for a membership? +
You can sign up for a membership by visiting our facility during business hours, contacting us through the website, or calling our customer service. We'll guide you through the available plans and help you choose the best option for your needs.
Can I freeze my membership? +
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. Contact our customer service team to arrange the freeze period. A small administrative fee may apply.

Training

How do I book a personal trainer? +
You can book a personal trainer through our reception desk or online booking system. We'll match you with a trainer based on your goals and schedule.
What should I bring to my first training session? +
Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a small towel. Your trainer will provide all necessary equipment for the session.

Classes

How do I sign up for classes? +
Classes can be booked through our mobile app or website. Premium and Elite members can book up to 7 days in advance, while Basic members can book 48 hours before the class.
What if I need to cancel a class? +
You can cancel a class up to 4 hours before its scheduled start time. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. You can also set up automatic monthly payments for your convenience.
How do refunds work? +
Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our customer service team to discuss your situation and available options.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions you might have.
